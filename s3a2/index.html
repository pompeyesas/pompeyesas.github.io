With the “Philadelphia Maneto” applet you wont forget to admire and appreciate the space and historic values of the City of Brotherly Love as you walk around Penn's campus.

Many people at Penn, whether native to Philadelphia or not, don’t know the rich history or values of the city, such as being the first city to have its own flag. Most people don't even know Philly has a flag, but PHM is here to change that!

With “PHM” anytime you come close to the Love sign on campus you will be reminded of the core value of this city; Philadelphia Maneto, which translates to “Let Brotherly Love Continue”
